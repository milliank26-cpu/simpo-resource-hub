Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

At Simpo, we value the privacy of our students and educators. This policy explains how we collect and protect your information within the Uganda Resource Hub.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a tailored academic experience, we collect:

  • Account Data: Name, email, and password.
  • Academic Profile: Your school name and current class (e.g., P.7, S.4, S.6) to provide relevant UNEB materials.
  • Usage Data: Which notes or past papers you download to help us improve our resources.
Simpo Promise: We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers. Your academic journey stays between you and the hub.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data specifically to:

  • Deliver curriculum-specific resources (UCE/UACE/PLE).
  • Send updates about new past papers or study guides.
  • Improve the navigation and speed of the Simpo platform.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption to ensure your school records and login credentials remain secure from unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

As a user in Uganda, you have the right to access your data, request corrections to your school/class information, or delete your account at any time via your profile settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy, please reach out via our Contact Page.
