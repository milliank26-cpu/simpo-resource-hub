Our Mission

SIMPO is a high-performance digital library built to simplify the **New Lower Secondary Curriculum (NLSC)** transition. We centralize verified NCDC Prototypes, editable Schemes of Work, and simplified Revision Notes. Our mission is to empower every Ugandan student to master 21st-century competencies through accessible, zero-friction digital tools.
